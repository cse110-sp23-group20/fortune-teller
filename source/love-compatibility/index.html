User Guide

Step 1: Selecting Zodiac Pairs

  • Position your mouse cursor over the left wheel.
  • Click and hold down the mouse button.
  • While keeping the button pressed, move your cursor along the wheel to rotate it.
    • Note: Alternatively, you can scroll up or down while hovering over the wheel to rotate it.
  • If you do not know your zodiac sign, rotate the wheel until the date range displayed in the middle of the wheel includes your birthday. The zodiac pointed to by the wheel's corresponding arrow is your sign.
  • Once the arrow is pointing to your zodiac sign, release the mouse button or stop scrolling to make your selection.
  • Repeat the same action on the right wheel to select your partner's zodiac sign using their birthday.

Step 2: Confirm Selection and Calculate Compatibility

  • After selecting you and your partner's zodiac sign, press the "Find Out" button located near the bottom of the page.
  • Wait for a moment as the compatibility calculation is processed.

Step 3: Interpret the Compatibility Results

  • The compatibility results will provide insights into the romantic compatibility between the selected zodiac signs.
  • From there, you can then press the "Back" button to return to the Zodiac Compatibility site.
